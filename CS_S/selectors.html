some facts about the life of A.P.J. Abdul Kalam:

Born on October 15, 1931 in Rameswaram, India, and died on July 27, 2015 in Shillong

A scientist and politician who played a key role in India'smissile and nuclear weapons programs. He was also the president of India from 2002 to 2007
